Background check is the act of reviewing both confidential and public information to investigate a person or entity's history. Background checks are commonly performed by employers to ensure that:

  1. An employee is who he or she says they are
  2. To determine that the individual does not have a damaging history (such as criminal activity)
  3. To confirm information that an applicant included on their application for employment
