We vet and check so you don’t have to

We are a background, medical, criminal vetting and fact finding service. We assist our clients in vetting all their staff; to ensure they know exactly who they are hiring and to ensure the safety of the most important people “’Their family “!

Our Story

As a domestic employer myself and a Human Resource director for over 20 years dealing with personnel and asking pertinent questions comes naturally, I realized we were less stringent and careful when hiring into our homes and my driver almost stealing my car, made me realize how careless and trusting we sometimes are.

I have come across and heard many stories of, dangerous and even criminal staff being hired and causing great harm to families, we also realized many times it was due to not doing a thorough check and as there was no database or any place to check or vet before you made that final decision. We must register, report and warn others from hiring these people. We could save many from harm, the trauma of theft, living with a potentially infectious or dangerous person and create awareness within the community it was with this passion DOMESTIREG was birthed

Why Domestireg

Over the years and as an employer of domestics ourselves, we have witnessed firsthand and heard many horror stories of theft, violence and loss of life, more significant is the maltreating of both infant and young male and female children, beating, violent shaking, sexual molestation, and even the untimely death of these innocents by people you bring into your home, trust and pay to take care of your family.

In the last year there’s been an increase in kidnappings of children, nannies and maids poisoning families out of spite, Drivers kidnapping their bosses or employers’ children, males posing as a female nanny. Chefs, ripping their employers off with incredibly overshot shopping lists and incessant stealing.

What we find most alarming is the number of these incidents and people don't report it!

What essentially happens is you let them go and do not report them, you are too horrified or traumatized by the incident or focused on reclaiming your lost or stolen property. What we don’t realize is they simply turn around and go to work for our friends, sister’s friends, another family member of yours or someone you may or may not know etc.

They move to other parts of your community, your city or simply move to another state and continue these crimes, causing serious loss, pain, heartbreak and hardships for other loving families, who sadly may even be related to us in one way or another. We can simply prevent this by reporting this on DOMESTIREG!

At Domestireg, you can quickly register, verify, and conduct medical and background checks on all your current and potential staff you are considering for hire.

You can also report incidents, upload pictures and offer recommendations for great staff. This will also help service providers to strive to be better and to deter them from evil acts knowing their pictures and information is out for the greater community to see, as well as deter others with evil intentions from committing their heinous acts.

More step to safeguarding our homes, personal sanctuaries and hard-earned belongings from potential criminals.

We work with a passion of taking challenges and ensuring a safe and healthy life for you and your family
